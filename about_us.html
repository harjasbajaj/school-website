Welcome to FireBird High School

Our School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Examination. An excellent result in the CBSE board examinations is a tradition that has become ingrained in the ethos of the school. The school is a proud recipients of the ‘INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL AWARD’ by THE BRITISH COUNCIL. We offer co-education from standards I to XII comprising education streams like Commerce, Humanities and Science at XI, XII level. The school is equipped with State-of-the-Art Infrastructure and trained and dedicated faculty for ensuring holistic development of the students.
